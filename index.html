ABOUT



I am a Ph.D. student with Robert Likamwa in the
Ira A. Fulton School of Engineering at Arizona State University - Tempe.

Before joining ASU, I obtained my Bachelor's degree at the University of Washington in Electrical Engineering in 2018 where I graduated with Honors. I conducted research with Shwetak Patel and Hanchuan Li in the Ubiquitous Computing Lab.

I am interested in enhancing human computer interaction experiences through mixed reality and machine learning, advancing education and encouraging overall health and wellness.
