ABOUT



As a Ph.D. candidate under the mentorship of Robert Likamwa in the Ira A. Fulton School of Engineering at Arizona State University - Tempe, I am committed to advancing the frontiers of human-computer interaction experiences using cutting-edge technologies such as mixed reality.

My passion for these fields was sparked during my Bachelor's studies in Electrical Engineering at the University of Washington, where I graduated with Honors in 2018. While there, I had the privilege of conducting pioneering research alongside trailblazers Shwetak Patel and Hanchuan Li in the Ubiquitous Computing Lab.

I aspire to leverage my knowledge and expertise to create transformative, immersive experiences for education and promote overall health and wellness.
